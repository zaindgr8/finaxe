Case Study

Gaming Studio/Mobile Gaming.

The Challenge

The client, a gaming studio with ambitions to compete globally, needed to build a world-class team to develop and publish mobile-first, free-to-play, and play-and-earn games. Key challenges included:

  • Attracting top talent from leading gaming studios worldwide.
  • Establishing a presence in both MENA and Europe.
  • Building a diverse team of developers, product managers, and business leaders to drive innovation and growth.

Client Overview

Industry:  Gaming Studio

Legal Structure:  Joint Venture Partnership with a Chinese Conglomerate

Inception Size:  2 Employees

Current Size:  200 Employees

Location:  Saudi Arabia, Europe

Inception Year:  2022

Finaxe Role:  Talent Acquisition and Organizational Structure Advisory

The Results

Global Team

200+ Employees: Built a team of 200 employees, including top talent from leading gaming studios.

Executive Leadership

Innovation & Growth: Recruited a world-class executive team to drive business expansion.

Market Presence

MENA & Europe: Established offices in key markets, positioning the client as a global competitor.

Product Development

Mobile-First Games: Launched high-quality games targeting a global user base.

The Solution

Finaxe provided end-to-end talent acquisition and organizational advisory services, including:

  • Leadership Hiring:

    Hired a Head of Studio and Head of Publishing to oversee game development and distribution.
    Sourced remote mobile gaming developers from the US, UK, Europe, and Asia.
    Recruited key roles such as Head of Product, CTO (Mobile Gaming), and Legal Counsel.

  • Leadership Recruitment:

    Recruited a Group CEO, Chief Product Officer (CPO), Chief Marketing Officer (CMO), and Head of HR.

  • Global Talent Acquisition:

    Attracted global talent to fill critical executive roles in mobile gaming and technology.

  • Investment and Expansion:

    Hired a Head of Investment & Acquisition and a Business Development Director for China.
    Established a head office studio in MENA and a new European office.

  • Product and User Experience:

    Recruited data analysts and game designers to define and manage the player experience.
    Conducted market research and competitor product analysis to inform game development.

Conclusion

Finaxe enabled the client to transform from a two-person startup into a globally competitive gaming studio. By attracting top talent and establishing
a strong organizational structure we helped the client achieve its vision of creating innovative
high-quality games for a global audience.
