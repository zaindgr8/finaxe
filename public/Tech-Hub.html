Tier 1 Global international Bank - Tech Hub

The Challenge

The client, a Tier 1 global bank, aimed to establish a tech hub in the MENA region to support its 10-year plan of hiring 1,000 employees for new products in treasury, wealth management, and personal banking. Key challenges included:

  • Mapping local tech talent in the MENA region.
  • Building a backend system to manage profiles and mandates efficiently.

The Solution

Finaxe provided comprehensive talent acquisition and market mapping services, including:

  • Market Mapping:

    Conducted a detailed analysis of local tech talent in the MENA region.
    Submitted the first 20 profiles across different seniority levels.

  • Leadership Hiring:

    Recruited key roles such as Head of IC Technology, FX Release Manager, and AVP Senior Database Developer.
    Hired VP and AVP team leaders for testing, DevOps, and business analysis.

  • Tech Recruitment:

    Sourced junior and senior developers for Python, C, and other programming languages.
    Recruited mobile developers (Android and iOS) and enterprise desktop engineers.

  • Product Expansion:

    Supported the development of new products in e-trading, markets & treasury, and wealth management.
