Specializations

Infrastructure Development

We specialize in recruiting for large-scale infrastructure projects, including highways, railways, airports, and bridges. Our candidates are experienced in all phases of development, from planning to execution.

Energy Projects

Our recruitment expertise extends to energy infrastructure projects such as solar farms, wind power installations, and oil & gas facilities. We support hiring for roles in design, construction, and project management.

Urban Planning

We have a strong track record of hiring for residential and commercial urban planning projects, including luxury developments, affordable housing, and mixed-use developments. We also specialize in hiring for smart city initiatives.

Industrial Complexes

Our team has extensive experience in recruiting for industrial projects such as manufacturing plants, warehouses, logistics hubs, and more. We hire for all levels of industrial design, construction, and management roles.

Technology-Driven Solutions

We recruit for roles in advanced technologies for construction, including Building Information Modeling (BIM), digital twins, and robotics for automation, efficiency, and safety on construction sites.

Mega/Giga Projects

Specialized recruitment for large-scale, transformative projects such as smart cities, large infrastructure projects, and mega industrial complexes. We help build teams that manage high-value, high-impact projects.

EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction)

We support recruitment for all roles in Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) projects. From engineering design and procurement to construction project management, we provide specialized talent.

Sustainable Construction

We recruit professionals skilled in green building, renewable energy integration, and eco-friendly materials. Our expertise includes roles in LEED certification, energy-efficient design, and carbon reduction for sustainable urban development, net-zero projects, and circular economy initiatives. We connect you with talent driving innovation and environmental responsibility in construction.
